Introduction

Daily Disconnect ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Daily Disconnect collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Daily disconnect session times and durations
  • Streak data (consecutive days of achieving your goal)
  • Daily goal preferences (your chosen disconnect time target)
  • Notification preferences (time windows and frequency)
  • App theme preferences (light/dark mode selection)
  • Monthly disconnect history

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by Daily Disconnect is:

  • Stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (UserDefaults)
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never used for analytics, tracking, or advertising
  • Used solely to provide core app functionality (tracking your disconnect sessions, maintaining streaks, and displaying progress)

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your data remains on your iPhone and is protected by iOS security features
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to iCloud or any cloud service
  • No Account Required: The app does not require account creation or login
  • Data Deletion: You can permanently delete all your data at any time through Settings → Data → Clear All Data

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Daily Disconnect uses iOS's built-in notification system to send you gentle reminders to disconnect. We do not track or store any information about notification delivery or interaction. All notification scheduling happens locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Daily Disconnect does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline and does not make any network requests.

Children's Privacy

Daily Disconnect does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the app (viewable in the Insights tab)
  • Delete all your data at any time (Settings → Data → Clear All Data)
  • Disable notifications (Settings → Notifications)
  • Uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data
